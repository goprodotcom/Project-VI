**Welcome to Nerdcon!**

Nerdcon is Miami's most popular anime and video game convention that occurs in the spring and fall seasons. Hosted by Funimation Entertainment, this convention boasts a wide assortment of competitions and autograph signings from popular faces within the media industry!

This year, Nerdcon is being held at the Hilton Miami Hotel on December 35th to December 18th, 9 A.M. to midnight. Tickets are $30 per adult, $25 per senior (ages 55+).
**Guests to Nerdcon must park in lots 3A-3D. Parking passes are given at the gate, cost is $15.
